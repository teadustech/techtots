Definition of a Computer:

A computer is an electronic device that processes data according to a set of instructions to perform various tasks. It can execute a wide range of operations, from simple calculations to complex computations, and it can store, retrieve, and manipulate data in various forms. Computers are used in almost every aspect of modern life, including education, business, entertainment, communication, and scientific research.
